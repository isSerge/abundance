Blockchain as a library
26 April 2025·7 mins
Most blockchain implementations are pieces of software that include the logic to support many different roles/features, possibly all at once: bootstrap node, block producer, RPC node, archival node, light client, etc. That is one way to do it, but one thing I learned over the years is that you can do a lot of interesting optimizations if you can apply additional constraints during the design phase. So why is basically everyone trying to combine all into one? Let&rsquo;s consider different roles separately first and see what is special or interesting about them.
